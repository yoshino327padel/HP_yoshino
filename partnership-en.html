Partnership Opportunities

Enhance your brand value through the fastest-growing sport in the world

Partnership Recruitment

Kushima Yoshino is looking for partner companies to support her competitive activities. We offer PR and promotional opportunities that contribute to enhancing your corporate value.

Padel is played by over 35 million people across 90+ countries and is one of the fastest-growing sports in the world. With padel growing in Japan too, becoming a partner now gives you first-mover brand recognition. Join Kushima Yoshino in the challenge to compete on the world stage.

Benefits for Partner Companies

Through our partnership, we provide the following benefits.

01

PR Through International Tournaments & Events

Gain exposure as a partner company at international tournaments, domestic events, and media coverage. Build connections between your company and sports fans through padel experience sessions and clinics.

02

Enhance Your Brand Value

Display your company name and logo on our official website, apparel, and SNS to boost brand awareness. Reach a wide audience of padel fans and sports enthusiasts across all demographics.

03

Social Contribution Through Athlete Support

Supporting an athlete who continues to challenge at international competitions contributes to sports promotion and the development of future generations. Enhance your company's social credibility through sports-related CSR activities.

04

Reach New Customer Segments & Talent

Expand corporate awareness among those interested in padel and sports. The spirit of taking on challenges enhances your brand image and can positively impact recruitment efforts.

Aiming for Victory on the World Stage

Kushima Yoshino started competing in padel in January 2024, and in just 9 months was selected for the Japanese national team. She won the Asian championship and finished 11th at the World Championships. Since 2025, she has been competing individually on the world tour, currently ranked 162nd.

Your support goes toward international tournament travel expenses, equipment costs, and training fees — all essential for competing on the world stage. Join us in supporting Yoshino's challenge to reach the top.
